My favorite Sites:

    Happy Cow

    Provides Vegetarian and Vegan Restaurants in the area. This site was a big help for me when I was a vegan and trying to find a good place to eat in the city or while visiting new areas.

    Voir Anime

    This site is for Anime shows and movies. It is often hard to find a good source for anime that has the original anime and I use this site from time to time.

    Geeks4Geeks

    This site has been very resourceful for me while studying computer science. I use it pretty much every semester. This is a popular site like Stack Overflow or W3W Schools.
